# Employee Churn & Retention Analysis 📊

An HR Data Analysis case study exploring the key drivers of employee attrition (churn) using SQL and Power BI.

## 💡 Key Insights
* **Pune Churn Rate:** **50.4%** attrition rate (compared to 26.7% in Bangalore).
* **Payment Tier 2 Attrition:** Employees in Tier 2 have an alarming **60.0%** resignation rate.
* **Gender Disparity:** Female employees leave at a much higher rate (**47.1%**) than male employees (**25.8%**).
* **Master's Degree Loss:** Employees with a Master's degree have a **48.8%** churn rate.
* **Bench Impact:** Being placed on the bench increases the likelihood of leaving to **45.4%** (vs. 33.1% for non-benched).

---

## 🎯 Recommendations
* **Pune Location Audit:** Conduct targeted exit interviews and review the management and work environment in Pune.
* **Review Tier 2 Compensation:** Re-evaluate and adjust the salary structure and incentives for Payment Tier 2.
* **Implement Female Retention Policies:** Introduce flexible working hours or remote work options to support female staff.
* **Create Master's Career Paths:** Design clear promotion tracks to keep highly educated talent engaged.
* **Optimize Bench Management:** Minimize bench time and provide active training during transition periods.
